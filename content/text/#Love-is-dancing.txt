/*I wrote Love is dancing about a DJ's house party that I wanted to go to. It was two hours away and hosted by someone I hooked up with 3 years prior. I impulsively told them that I would go before I really had any plans to. Eventually, I dragged my friends along (they didn't really like the party as much as I did) and tried my best to enjoy it through the guilt of basically forcing them to go. Anyways, the song was written out of admiration. How so much of the community there was embracing their identity and how they were all brought together through the local, queer music scene.

A lot of the lyrics were taken from a poem I wrote during the Summer of 2025, when I was finally ready to release music under my own name (RIP mompolish). Daily, I would go out and walk around my apartment complex for hours. I walked past the same architecture, trees, people, roads, etc. every single day, watching them change with time. It gave me a lot of time to think about who I wanted to be and present myself as (enter in "DANCE").

This song is my way of professing my love for music. I see it in everything.*/

/*I wrote 'Love is dancing' about a DJ's house party that I wanted to go to. It was two hours away in Cincy and I impulsively told the host that I would go before I really had any plans to. Eventually, I dragged my friends along (they didn't end up liking the party) and tried my best to enjoy it through the guilt of basically forcing them to go. The song was written out of admiration. How so much of the community there was embracing their identity and how they were all brought together through the local, queer music scene.

This song is a profession of love for music. I see it in everything.*/

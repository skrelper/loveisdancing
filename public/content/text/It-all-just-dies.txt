/*I made this song with Etiyenne and it was so much fun because we just played a loop from my Minilogue and kept adding things over it. I thought the little noise in the beginning sounded like a Pokemon and that also jumpstarted the story of the music video (eventually reworked the idea into something less Pokemon-like).

I decided to use it as an EP closer, writing all the lyrics about how the rest of the world just disappears when I'm listening to really good music. That's also why I wanted to make it a "HeartBeat Reprise" of sorts, incorporating the two previous songs into it and I think that made it feel like a celebration.*/

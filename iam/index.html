iam...

iam... is an interactive audiovisual machine. It translates an audio input into a visual output through in-depth audio analysis.

Me speaking into the microphone with iam... reflecting off of me from a mirror. A QR code for the Prompt Machine is visible in the background. Photo courtesy of Claire Brown.

Matt interacting with the iam... installation.

Besides iam..., the Chroma experience featured fluroescent yarn installations and a pin-up board asking viewers what sound they would be.

On May 15, 2026, it was exhibited at Columbus College of Art & Design's annual show, Chroma, as part of the Graphic Design program's senior capstone projects.
